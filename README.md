# Globetakers / Multistream Website

A website I built for my mom's business, Globetakers, to help her reach more customers and let people order her Multistream products directly through WhatsApp.

**Live Demo:** [www.multistreamglobetakers.com](http://www.multistreamglobetakers.com)

## Why I Built This

My mom runs a small business as a Multistream distributor under Globetakers. For a long time, she relied on word of mouth and in-person conversations to find new customers and take orders. I wanted to give her something better — a real online presence that worked as hard as she does.

So I built her this website. The goal was simple: anyone who hears about her business should be able to find her online, see what she sells, and place an order without friction. Every "Order Now" and "Become a Distributor" button on the site opens a pre-filled WhatsApp message straight to her phone, so there's no form to fill out, no account to create, and no awkward back-and-forth before the conversation starts. She just gets a clear message and knows exactly where the customer came from.

This project means a lot to me because it's the first thing I've built that someone I love uses every day. It pushed me to think beyond just writing code — I had to consider who the user actually is (her customers, many of whom aren't tech-savvy), what device they're on (almost always a phone), and how to make the path from "interested" to "ordered" as short as possible.

## What I Learned

Building this taught me that good design isn't about looking impressive — it's about removing friction for the person on the other end. I rewrote the layout multiple times based on watching my mom and her customers actually try to use it. Small things mattered: making buttons big enough to tap easily, keeping the WhatsApp links pre-filled with the right message, and making sure the site loaded fast on slow connections.

I also learned how to ship something real and maintain it. When my mom asked for changes — a new product, a different phone number, updated pricing — I had to make those updates quickly without breaking anything.

## Built With

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript

No frameworks, no build step — just clean, hand-written code that loads fast and is easy to maintain.

This site isn't a tutorial project or a clone. It's a real product, used by a real business, that has helped my mom turn casual interest into actual orders. For me, it's proof that I can take a problem someone I care about has, sit down, and build something that solves it.
